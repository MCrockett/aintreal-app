Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 26, 2025

AIn't Real ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our game.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide the game experience:

  • Account Information: If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from those services. This is used to identify you and save your game statistics.
  • Guest Play: If you play as a guest, we generate a random display name. No personal information is collected.
  • Game Statistics: We store your game scores, win/loss records, and gameplay statistics to display on your profile.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the game
  • Save your progress and statistics
  • Display your name to other players in multiplayer games
  • Improve the game experience

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • Other Players: Your display name is visible to other players in multiplayer games.
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and Cloudflare for hosting. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Cloudflare's infrastructure. We retain your account information and game statistics for as long as your account is active.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Play as a guest without creating an account
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Sign out at any time from the app

Image Content

The real photographs used in the game are sourced from Pexels under their license. AI-generated images are created specifically for this game. No user-uploaded images are used.

Children's Privacy

AIn't Real is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
